About This Property

Traditional Maharashtrian-style hilltop villa in Amboli, surrounded by lush forests, mountains and river views. Enjoy peaceful mornings with bird songs, a spacious veranda, dining space, bonfire evenings, and panoramic scenery.

Two bedrooms with attached washrooms, living room, fully equipped kitchen, and a scenic Madi room. Ideal for families and groups.
